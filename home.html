WHO WE ARE


Enactus ESTC is a group of passionate students from the superior school of technology which is part of the University Hassan 2 in Casablana. We are committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and create a better, more sustainable world. Our team of over 90 is dedicated to transforming communities, creating innovative solutions, inspiring entrepreneurship, and generating economic opportunities. As diverse and different as each one of us may be, we are all here for one common goal!

OUR WORK

Our Enactus program is built around our projects. We are committed to applying our passions, talents, and ideas to impact as many lives as we can. We do not simply hand out help to people in need but work side-by-side with them to create opportunities, ensuring every person and community we touch is empowered to live up to its fullest potential.
